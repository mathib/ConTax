Abstract

The ConTax ontology contains additional terminology for structuring and annotating RDFS/OWL taxonomies for describing constructions (components, materials, spatial zones, damages, construction tasks and properties). It also functions as an index for known taxonomies starting from root classes and properties.

The namespace for ConTax terms is https://w3id.org/con-tax#

The suggested prefix for the ConTax namespace is contax

The Turtle version of the ConTax ontology is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/contax.ttl
The RDF/XML version of the ConTax ontology ontology is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/contax.rdf
The JSON-LD version of the ConTax ontology ontology is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/contax.jsonld
The N-Triples version of the ConTax ontology ontology is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/contax.nt

The ConTax ontology was created by Mathias Bonduel (KU Leuven) during his PhD research (2017-2020) funded by the Research Foundation Flanders in the form of a Strategic Basic Research grant (grant agreement 1S65917N).
